This is a list of singles that charted in the top ten of the Billboard Hot 100, an all-genre singles chart, in 2015.

During 2015, Mark Ronson's "Uptown Funk" (featuring Bruno Mars) spent 31 weeks in the top 10, becoming the single with the longest run in the top 10 of 2015 and the second longest running top 10 single of all time.[1] Taylor Swift received five top 10 hits during the year, all from her fifth studio album 1989. The Weeknd and Justin Bieber followed her with four, with Fetty Wap and Meghan Trainor receiving three.
