From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Chart published weekly by Billboard

The Artist 100 is a chart published weekly by Billboard in the United States. The Billboard Artist 100 combines performance across the Hot 100 chart, the Billboard 200 album chart, and the Internet-centric Social 50 chart.[1]

The Artist 100 chart premiered in the issue dated July 19, 2014, with Trey Songz ranked number one.[2]
