Record sales chart in China

The Sino Chart was a sales chart in China established in 2009.[1][2][3] Chart rankings were based on physical record sales (album, EPs and singles), and did not include download sales.[4]

Charts were published every Sunday for paid members and, on Mondays, were released on their official website.

On April 26, 2015, the site officially closed.
