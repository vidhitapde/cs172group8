Latin music award honoring women

Billboard Latin Women in Music is an annual event presented by Billboard magazine for "groundbreaking women who are shaping the future of Latin music".[1] The event was created in 2023 as a "response to an exploding Latin music business that crossed borders and continents but was united in language and repertoire".[2] Since its inception, the event has been broadcast by Telemundo.[1]

Ana Gabriel, Emilia, Evaluna Montaner, Goyo, Thalía, and Shakira were among the first honorees at the inaugural event, with the latter being named Woman of the Year.[3] Karol G and Selena Gomez, who have won Woman of the Year in 2024 and 2025 respectively,[4][5] were also named Woman of the Year at the all-genre Billboard Women in Music awards in 2024 and 2017, respectively.[6][7]
