Marcin Przybyłowicz (born (1985-05-04)4 May 1985)[1] is a Polish composer and sound designer. He is mostly known for as the lead composer for The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt and Cyberpunk 2077.[2] He also composed the score to the Polish television historical drama Korona królów.[3] He was born on the day of the May 1985 lunar eclipse.
