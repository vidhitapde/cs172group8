Multi-system emulators

This section is missing information about list of supposed consoles. Please expand the section by making an edit requestto include this information. Further details may exist on the talk page. (February 2021)

Multi-system emulators are capable of emulating the functionality of multiple systems.

  • higan
  • MAME (Multiple Arcade Machine Emulator)
  • Mednafen
  • MESS (Multi Emulator Super System), formerly a stand-alone application and now part of MAME
  • OpenEmu
