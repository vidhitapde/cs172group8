Sambo
at the 2018 Asian Games
VenueJakarta Convention Center
Dates31 August – 1 September 2018
Competitors71 from 18 nations

Sambo at the 2018 Asian Games was held at the Jakarta Convention Center Assembly Hall, Jakarta, Indonesia from 31 August to 1 September.[1]
