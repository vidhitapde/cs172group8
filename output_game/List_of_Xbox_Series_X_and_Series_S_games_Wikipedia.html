The following is a list of games that have been announced for release or released on the Xbox Series X and Xbox Series S. Both were released on November 10, 2020.

The Xbox Series X and Series S have full backward compatibility with Xbox One games as well as several Xbox 360 and original Xbox games that were supported on the Xbox One, excluding those that use Kinect.[1] Most Xbox One games that also have an Xbox Series X/S version will automatically download the Xbox Series X/S version for the system via Microsoft's "Smart Delivery" program, without an additional purchase for both physical and digital game purchases. Physical games are sold on Blu-ray and digital games can be purchased through the Microsoft Store. This list excludes backward compatible games.

There are currently 942 games on this list.[a]
