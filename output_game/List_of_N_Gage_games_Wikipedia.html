From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Left to right: Nokia N-Gage, Nokia N-Gage QD

The N-Gage is a PDA-like device that combined features of a cell phone and a handheld game console, developed and designed by Nokia and released on October 7, 2003.[1] The following lists contains all of the known games released for the N-Gage, as well as unreleased games. Only 58 of the games developed for the system received a North American retail release, with two retail releases being exclusive for PAL territories. However, a few titles only saw release through either digital distribution or bundles that came inside a MMC movie card.[2][3][4] In addition, one more game, Space Impact Evolution X, came with the system on CD-ROM that was later made available for Symbian S60v2 cell phones.
