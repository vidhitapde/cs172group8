TopSpin 2K25 is a 2024 tennis video game, developed by Hangar 13 and published by 2K. It is part of the Top Spin video game series. The game was released for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S on 26 April 2024, and is the first installment since the original to not be released on a Nintendo console.[1][2][3] It received mixed reviews from critics.

Development

[edit]

TopSpin 2K25 is developed by the Czech subsidiary of Hangar 13.[4][5][6] Electronic musician BT composes the score for the game.[7]

TopSpin 2K25 received 'mixed or average" reviews from critics for the PC and Xbox Series X/S versions, while the PS5 version received "generally favorable" reviews, according to review aggregator website Metacritic.[8] In Japan, four critics from Famitsu gave the game a total score of 32 out of 40, with each critic awarding the game an 8 out of 10.[9] It was nominated for "Best Sports / Racing Game" at The Game Awards 2024.[11]
