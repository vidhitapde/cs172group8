Device used for physical activity

Exercise equipment is any apparatus or device used during physical activity[1] to enhance the strength or conditioning effects of that exercise by providing either fixed or adjustable amounts of resistance, or to otherwise enhance the experience or outcome of an exercise routine.[2][3]

Exercise equipment may also include such wearable items as proper footgear, gloves, and hydration packs.
