The Nokia 8600 Luna is a mobile phone produced by Nokia. It has a stainless steel body, and weighs 140 grams.[1]

Features include: TFT display (240 x 320 pixels), 2 megapixel camera, video recording and streaming, digital music player, and FM radio.[1][2][3]
