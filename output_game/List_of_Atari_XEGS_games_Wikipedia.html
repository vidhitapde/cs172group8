From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Atari XEGS, released in 1987, is the final member of the Atari 8-bit computers. This list only contains games released by Atari Corporation during the XEGS's lifetime, all of which use "Atari XE Video Game Cartridge" packaging;[1][2] many are earlier floppy disk-based releases converted to ROM cartridge. The XEGS is compatible with all Atari 8-bit computer software, whether labeled for XEGS or not.
